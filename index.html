My introduction

Born and brought up in the city of Lucknow, I completed my secondary education in 2015 from Sherwood Academy (ICSE board), and higher secondary education in 2017 from City Montessori School(ISC) scoring 75% with Computer Science as one of my major subjects. By the time I had completed higher secondary education, I had already gained beginner level coding skills and became fluent in basic coding languages like C++ and JAVA, during higher secondary education. Following my dedication and achieving decent grades in Computer Science being a motivator, I went on to pursue Bachelors in Computer Applications from Galgotias University, and it was during my bachelors that I was able to figure out my passion for web development among other branches or domains in Computer Science. As part of my Bachelors curriculum, I was introduced to basic web development which really increased my inclination towards it.
